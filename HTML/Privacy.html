Privacy Policy

What Information We Collect

Personal Information

- Name and email address
- Billing and shipping address
- Payment details (processed securely through third-party providers)
- Phone number (for shipping updates)

Automatically Collected

- IP address and browser type
- Pages visited on our site
- Games viewed or purchased
- Cookies (see section below)

Cookies

We use cookies to:
- Remember your cart items
- Keep you logged in
- Track site usage for improvements
You can disable cookies in browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share only what's necessary with:
- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Shipping carriers (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
- Analytics providers (Google Analytics)

Data Security

- 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
- No credit card storage on our servers
- Regular security audits

Your Rights

- Access your personal data anytime
- Request deletion of your account
- Opt out of marketing emails
- Export your purchase history

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. All accounts require parental consent for users under 18.

Changes to Policy

We'll notify users via email of any material changes to this policy.
